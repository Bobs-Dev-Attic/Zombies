How to Play

  • Move & Aim — left virtual stick (or WASD / arrows). You face the way you move; your aim holds when you stop.
  • Shoot / Swing — hold FIRE (or Space / click). Your weapon fires or swings in the direction you're facing — no auto-aim. On desktop, point with the mouse to aim.
  • Sprint — push the stick fully; drains stamina. Low stamina & heavy wounds slow you.
  • Reload — ⟳ button (or R).
  • Swap weapon — ↔ button (or Q / mouse wheel).
  • Interact — ✋ to open/close doors and grab pickups (or E). Walk over items to auto-grab. Shut doors to block the horde.
  • Dismember them — blow or smash off limbs: legless zombies crawl prone, and fewer arms mean weaker hits.
  • Survive the waves, gather weapons, and reach the EXIT to advance the setting.
